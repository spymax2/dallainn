Founded in 2024, The Porridge Center started with a simple mission: to redefine breakfast. We believe porridge shouldn't be bland or boring. It should be a hearty, wholesome, and delicious experience that powers your day. We use only locally-sourced, organic grains and fresh ingredients to craft every bowl.

From humble beginnings in a small market stall, we've grown into a beloved community spot known for our unique flavors and warm atmosphere.
